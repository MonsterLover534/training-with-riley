Questions

Frequently asked

Do I need a gym membership? +
Nope! Your program can be built for home, gym, or both — just let me know what equipment you have access to and I'll take care of the rest.
What if I'm a complete beginner? +
You're in exactly the right place. Programs are fully customized to your fitness level, so whether you've never worked out before or you're getting back into it, everything is built for where you are right now.
How do the weekly check-ins work? +
Each week we'll connect (via message or a short call — whatever works for you) to review your progress, talk through how things are going, and adjust your plan if needed.
How do I pay and get my program? +
After you reach out and we've discussed your goals, I'll send a payment link. Once payment is received your custom program is delivered within 2–3 business days.
Can I extend my program when it's done? +
Absolutely! Many clients choose to continue with a new program once they finish. We'll reassess your goals and build the next phase together.
Is this only for women? +
Yes — Training with Riley is a women-only coaching space, designed to feel supportive, safe, and judgment-free.
